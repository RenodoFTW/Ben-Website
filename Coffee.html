All drinks are available with milk alternatives.
Latte

A delicious coffee with double shots of espresso, topped with steamed milk and a layer of frothed milk to make it creamy.

Flat White

A single shot of espresso blended with micro-foamed milk which is then steamed to create a smooth and velvety coffee to enjoy.

Mocha

Strong espresso coffee mixed with our fairtrade chocolate powder and blended with steamed milk to create a sweet and homely cup of coffee.

Cappuccino

An espresso based coffee with equal parts espresso, steamed milk and foam. Variations of this coffee include topping it off with chocolate or cinnamon powder.

Americano

A classic espresso diluted with hot water without any cream or milk to elevate the rich and earthy flavour of the espresso coffee within.

Espresso

Boiling water poured onto finely ground and roasted fairtrade coffee beans increasing the intensity of the coffee's flavour.
