When we were founded in 1981, we started with the aim of creating a local community around a single location. Now in 2022, Bean & Brew aim to provide our customers and community with fresh and fairtrade goods such as coffee or lunch sandwiches. We strive to create new experiences and educate our customers on our specialties like baking. Our biggest mission is extending our influence by making our services convinient to access and fresh with new trends.

Founded in 1981 by coffee enthusiast Oscar Thomas, Bean & Brew served the local community within Harrogate being one of the first establishments to sell fairtrade coffee to its customers. Over the years, we have continued to grow branching out to nearby cities such as Leeds. As a result, we also expanded our services branching out to baking classes, small cuisine restaurants and our prestigious bakery takeaway.
